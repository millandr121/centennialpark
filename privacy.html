Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Eileen Scott Centennial Park ("we", "us") runs this website to take camping and moorage inquiries and bookings. We keep the information you give us to a minimum, use it only to serve you, and never sell it. This policy explains what we collect and your rights under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

What we collect

Only what you type into our inquiry, booking, or contact forms:

  • Your name and email address (and phone number, if you provide one).
  • Booking details: the dates, sites, party size, boat length, and any notes you add.
  • Your message, if you write to us through the contact form.

We do not use advertising cookies or track you across other websites. We collect no payment-card details on this site — payment is arranged separately (e-transfer or on arrival).

Why we collect it

To reply to your inquiry, hold and manage your reservation, send you a confirmation, and keep accurate booking and income records (which Canadian tax law requires us to retain for roughly six years).

Who we share it with

We share your information only with the service providers that make the website and its emails work, and only as needed to deliver them:

  • Cloudflare — hosts the website and stores submissions; also provides the "verify you are human" check that protects the forms from bots.
  • Resend — delivers our notification and confirmation emails.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone, ever.

How long we keep it

Booking and income records are kept for about six years to meet tax-record requirements. Raw inquiry and contact messages are kept only as long as useful and are periodically cleared.

Your rights

You can ask us what personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it (where we are not required to keep it for tax purposes). Just contact us using the details below.
